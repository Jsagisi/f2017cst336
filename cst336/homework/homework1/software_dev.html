Description

Computer networks are critical in many businesses which is why Network Administrators have several responsibilities. They install, support and organize the local area networks, wide area networks, as computer systems and other data systems. Network Administrators also organize the servers and desktop equipment as well as make sure email and the data storage network works properly. They can even manage telecommunication networks, so Network Administrators overall have a lot duties at their hands.
